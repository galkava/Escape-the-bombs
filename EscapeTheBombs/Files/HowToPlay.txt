Welcome!

1. In this game, you have to destroy 9 of 10 bombs by moving the tank and bringing them to hit one another.
2. You got one minute to achieve this aim.
3. At the beginning (before you press the Start button), you can position the tank where you want.
4. In emergency case you can use Jump option by pressing left or right Ctrl button. You got only three jumps.
5. Every destroyed bomb brings you 100 points.
6. Using the Jump option decrease your score by 50 points.
7. Is there only one bomb left, you won.
8. If one of the bombs hits the tank, you lose.

Good luck!
